Gallery Bench



Gallery Bench in steel, coated with boiled linseed oil paint, with a leather upholstered seat.



Column Dresser




Column dresser in solid cherry and steel with solid poplar drawers. Wood has an oiled finish. Steel drawer fronts, side and back panels are coated in linseed oil paint, hot rolled steel drawer pulls are left raw. Painting by Elise Dean Wolf.



floating nighstand





Prototype nighstand in solid cherry. Finished with boiled linseed oil.



It floats in dreams.

Steel and Oak Bar


Bar/console table in solid white oak and steel. Ebonized tabletop and a hard-wax oil finish.


aluminum body nightstand


Nightstand with an oxy acet welded aluminum frame, ebonized red oak and white oak table tops.


Stool 60 homage



An iteration on Alvar Aalto's Stool 60. The steam bent laminated legs, and seat are made from white oak.
