About

Liam Davis (American, b. 1999) is a Baltimore-based artist specializing in sculpture and photography. He was born in Arizona and grew up in Oregon, his work delves into themes of perception and invention in material matter. Davis builds furniture for a living and is a graduate of the Maryland Institute College of Art (MICA), where he studied both Interdisciplinary Sculpture and Photography. He worked as a research assistant in MICAâ€™s Biofabrication Lab, and he was a part of the Living Records team that won the 2025 Biodesign Challenge Outstanding Art prize.

His work has been presented his work in solo and group exhibitions including Living With It at GOYA Contemporary and Pitted: Home at the Heart of an Empire at Area 405 in Baltimore.

He is captivated by the way small parts can come together to form a greater whole.
